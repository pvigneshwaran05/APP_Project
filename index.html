Energy Saving Tips

1. Use Energy-Efficient Appliances

Upgrade to appliances with the ENERGY STAR label to save electricity and reduce your utility bills.

2. Turn Off Lights When Not in Use

Remember to turn off lights and electronics when leaving a room to conserve energy.

3. Adjust Thermostat Settings

Set your thermostat to an energy-saving temperature when you're away or asleep to save on heating and cooling costs.

4. Unplug Chargers and Devices

Unplug chargers and electronic devices when they're not in use to prevent energy wastage.
